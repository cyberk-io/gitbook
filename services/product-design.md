## How It Works

1. **Kickoff Call.** We sit down with you to understand your vision, your users, and your market. Our CEO leads this session.
2. **Research & Strategy.** (Professional & AIO) We study your competitors, analyze the market, and define your positioning.
3. **Design Sprint.** Our design team creates UX/UI based on the strategy. You review, give feedback, we iterate.
4. **Delivery.** You receive all deliverables — Figma files, landing page (Pro+), marketing content (AIO). Everything is documented and ready.
5. **Next Step.** If you want to build, we move seamlessly into Product Build. Your design is already optimized for our development framework.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need Product Design before Product Build?**

Product Design is strongly recommended but not required. If you already have a complete design (Figma files, user flows, specs), you can go straight to Product Build. However, most clients find that going through our design process first leads to a smoother, faster, and more cost-effective build.

**Q: Can I just buy Basic and build with another company?**

Absolutely. The deliverables are yours — you own everything. However, our designs are optimized for the Cyberk Framework, which means building with us is typically faster and more efficient than handing off to a third party.

**Q: What if I already have a designer but need strategic advisory?**

Our CEO advisory is included in every package. If you have design capabilities but need product strategy, competitor analysis, and market positioning — the Professional package gives you exactly that, plus we refine and validate your existing design.

**Q: What happens after Product Design is complete?**

You have three options:
1. **Move to Product Build** — We develop your designed product in 30 days. Most natural next step.
2. **Take the deliverables** — Use them to build in-house or with another team.
3. **Pause and revisit** — Your design and research remain yours. Come back when you're ready.

**Q: How involved do I need to be?**

We need you for the kickoff call and 2–3 review sessions (about 1 hour each). Beyond that, we handle everything. You approve milestones, and we keep you updated throughout.

**Q: Is the landing page in Professional a real, working website?**

Yes. It's a coded, responsive, live-ready website — not just a Figma mockup. We design it, build it, and deploy it. You can use it immediately for investor pitches, waitlists, or early user acquisition.

**Q: How does the AIO marketing content work?**

We start creating social content, articles, and video teasers in parallel with the design work. By the time your design is delivered, you already have a content calendar, branded social accounts, and initial posts published. The goal: when your product launches, people are already paying attention.
