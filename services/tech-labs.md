# Tech Labs

> Your integrated technology team. Long-term partnership, dedicated engineers, seamless collaboration.

## Overview

Some projects need more than a sprint — they need a team. Tech Labs gives you dedicated Cyberk engineers who embed into your workflow, understand your codebase, and ship continuously. No recruitment headaches, no onboarding delays, no management overhead.

This is Cyberk's long-term partnership model. Your engineers work exclusively on your project, join your standups, use your tools, and feel like part of your team — because they are.

## Why Tech Labs?

- **Zero ramp-up for Cyberk clients.** If you came through Product Build, your Tech Labs team already knows the codebase. No handoff friction, no knowledge transfer delays.
- **Cyberk-quality talent.** Every engineer is trained in the Cyberk Framework, practices our craftsman culture (Nghệ), and brings Web3 expertise by default.
- **Flexible scaling.** Start with 1 engineer, scale to 5. Add a designer mid-project. Adjust monthly based on your needs.
- **Management included.** A Cyberk project lead coordinates your team, runs sprints, and ensures delivery. You get output, not overhead.
- **CTO support.** Access to Cyberk's leadership for architecture reviews, technology decisions, and strategic product advice.

## How It Works

1. **Team Design.** We understand your project, tech stack, and pace. We recommend the right team composition.
2. **Onboarding.** Your dedicated team members join your project tools (Slack, Jira, GitHub, etc.) and ramp up on your codebase.
3. **Sprint Cycle.** We follow your development process — or bring ours. Weekly demos, bi-weekly sprints, daily standups — whatever works for you.
4. **Monthly Review.** Every month, we review progress, discuss roadmap, and adjust team composition if needed.
5. **Scale or Transition.** Need more people? Add them. Need fewer? Scale down. Project done? We handle the knowledge transfer.

## FAQ

**Q: What's the minimum engagement?**

3 months. Shorter engagements don't allow enough time for meaningful ramp-up and delivery. For shorter needs, consider Product Build instead.

**Q: Can I interview the engineers before they join my project?**

Yes. We present candidates based on your tech stack and project needs. You interview them, and we only proceed when you're satisfied with the fit.

**Q: How does communication work?**

Your Cyberk team members join your communication tools (Slack, Discord, Teams) and attend your standups. A Cyberk project lead serves as your single point of contact for operational needs. We work in your timezone overlap (minimum 4 hours daily).

**Q: What happens if an engineer needs to be replaced?**

If for any reason an engineer isn't the right fit, we replace them within 2 weeks at no extra cost. Knowledge transfer between the outgoing and incoming team member is handled by us.

**Q: Can I scale the team up or down?**

Yes. You can add or reduce team members on a monthly basis with 2 weeks notice. This gives you the flexibility to scale with your product's needs.

**Q: Do I own the code my Tech Labs team produces?**

100% yes. All code, documentation, and artifacts produced by your Tech Labs team belong to you. No IP restrictions, no licensing.

**Q: How is Tech Labs different from Product Build?**

Product Build is a fixed-price, fixed-scope, 30-day sprint — ideal for MVPs and new products. Tech Labs is an ongoing team engagement — ideal for continuous development, maintenance, and scaling. Many clients start with Product Build, then transition to Tech Labs for ongoing growth.

**Q: What if I started Product Build and want to transition to Tech Labs?**

Seamless. The same engineers who built your product can continue as your Tech Labs team. Zero ramp-up, zero knowledge loss. This is one of the biggest advantages of starting with Cyberk.

**Q: Is there any management fee on top of the per-person rates?**

Project management is included. A Cyberk project lead coordinates your team, runs sprints, and ensures delivery — at no additional cost. You pay the per-person rates, and we handle the rest.
