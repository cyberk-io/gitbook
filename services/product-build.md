## Requirement Changes & Add-ons

We understand that products evolve. When requirements change during or after the build:

**Small changes (1–2 features):**
- Quoted as fixed-price add-ons (starting from ~$5,000 per feature)
- Same transparent pricing — you know the cost before we start
- Added to the existing timeline or delivered in a follow-up sprint

**Larger ongoing development:**
- Transition to our [Tech Labs](tech-labs.md) service for dedicated, long-term development
- Your existing team already knows the codebase — zero ramp-up time
- Flexible monthly engagement

This ensures you always have a clear, cost-effective path forward — whether it's a quick addition or a long-term partnership.

## How It Works

1. **Scope Review.** We review your design and requirements. If you came through Product Design, scope is already defined. If not, we do a scoping session (~1 week).
2. **Sprint Planning.** We break the project into weekly sprints with clear milestones. You approve the plan before we start coding.
3. **Development.** Our team builds in weekly sprints. You get a demo every week to see progress and give feedback.
4. **QA & Testing.** Thorough testing — unit tests, integration tests, and user acceptance testing.
5. **Deployment.** We deploy to production, configure infrastructure, set up monitoring.
6. **Monitoring.** 15 or 30 days (based on package) of active monitoring. We watch for issues, fix bugs, and ensure stability.

## FAQ

**Q: How do you scope a $29K project? What's included?**

Every Product Build starts with a scope review. We define exactly what's included: number of screens/pages, smart contracts, API endpoints, integrations. Anything beyond the agreed scope is quoted as an add-on.

If you go through Product Design first ($5–8K), the scope is already crystal clear — your PRD becomes the contract.

**Q: What if my project is bigger than what $29K covers?**

The $29K package is designed for standard Web3 MVPs (10–20 screens, 2–5 smart contracts, standard integrations). For larger projects, we provide a custom quote based on your scope — still fixed price, still transparent.

Alternatively, you can build the MVP at $29K, validate with real users, then expand through add-ons or Tech Labs.

**Q: Do I own the source code?**

100% yes. All source code, smart contracts, documentation, and infrastructure configs belong to you. No lock-in, no licensing fees.

**Q: What happens after the monitoring period ends?**

Three options:
1. **Self-manage** — You have the code, docs, and infrastructure. Your team takes over.
2. **Add-on monitoring** — Extend monitoring on a monthly basis.
3. **Tech Labs** — Transition to a dedicated Cyberk team for ongoing development & maintenance.

**Q: Can I bring my own design?**

Absolutely. If you have production-ready Figma files with proper specs and design system, we can skip Product Design and go straight to Build. We'll do a design review during scoping to flag any issues.

**Q: How do weekly demos work?**

Every Friday, we demo what was built that week. You see the actual product running (not slides or mockups). You give feedback, we adjust in the next sprint. This keeps the project on track and avoids surprises at delivery.

**Q: What does "2 rounds of revision" mean?**

After initial delivery, you have 2 rounds to request changes and refinements within the original scope. This covers UI tweaks, bug fixes, and minor adjustments. Changes beyond the original scope are quoted as add-ons.

**Q: Is the Pro package worth the extra $6K?**

If you're building a product that handles real money (DeFi), real assets (RWA), or sensitive data — the Pro package pays for itself. CTO advisory during development catches architectural mistakes early. The security audit alone can prevent exploits that cost far more than $6K. Extended monitoring gives you 30 days of peace of mind post-launch.
